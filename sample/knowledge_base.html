Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I contact customer support?

    You can contact our customer support team through the **Live Chat** feature in the dashboard, or by submitting a support ticket. Our AI assistant is available 24/7, and human agents are online during business hours.

  • What is the average lifespan of a solar panel?

    Most modern solar panels have a lifespan of **25 to 30 years**. Their efficiency will gradually decrease over time, but they will continue to produce energy.

  • How can I track my energy usage?

    You can monitor your energy production and usage directly from your dashboard. We also provide a detailed breakdown in your monthly invoice and a detailed article on how to read your Solaready bill.
